About CMMS

Modern Apartment Maintenance Platform

Our Complaint and Maintenance Management System is designed specifically for apartment complexes. We bridge the gap between residents who need maintenance help and skilled workers who can provide it.

With real-time tracking, transparent communication and a rating system, we ensure every maintenance issue gets resolved quickly and professionally.

What We Offer

Key Features

Everything you need to manage apartment maintenance efficiently in one platform.

Easy Complaint Submission

Residents can submit maintenance complaints in seconds with title, description, category, priority and location.

Real-Time Status Tracking

Track your complaint from submission to resolution. Get notified at every stage of the process.

Skilled Worker Network

Access a network of verified plumbers, electricians, carpenters, cleaners and more maintenance workers.

Smart Notifications

Automatic notifications when a worker is assigned or when your complaint status changes.

Worker Rating System

Rate workers after job completion. Ratings help maintain quality and reward top performers.

Secure & Reliable

All accounts are verified. Worker registrations are approved by admin before they can start working.
